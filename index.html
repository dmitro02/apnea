This is an apnea training tool based on the method described by Australian freediver Adam Stern - video.

  1. Prepare to hold your breath.
  2. Hold your breath while pressing the "Start" button.
  3. Stop holding your breath while pressing the "Record" button.
  4. Rest until the end of the round and prepare for the next hold.
  5. Hold your breath as the next round begins.
  6. Repeat until the end of a session, or as long as you want.

Take it easy. Remember, all human records are worth nothing.
